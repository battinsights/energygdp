Energy Intensity Over Time

Energy intensity = EJ per trillion 2021 int. $ GDP PPP · Lower = more energy-efficient economy

Primary energy = total energy before conversion losses. Final energy = what end-users consume. Energy intensity (EJ per T$ GDP PPP) measures how much energy an economy uses per unit of output — declining intensity signals improved efficiency or structural shift toward services.
